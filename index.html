Our Strategic Intent

2UFoods' value offering is a township and rural market convenience store that is superior to its local competition in price and proximity. An integrated supply chain combined with opportunities for young black entrepreneurs will alleviate the financial stress of those who live in under-served communities. Our value proposition is centred on providing goods and meals at affordable prices at consumers convenience.
